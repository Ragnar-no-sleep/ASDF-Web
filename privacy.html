Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how ASDF Ecosystem ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the ASDF mobile application ("App").

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly. This policy applies to users of the ASDF Android application distributed through Google Play Store and Solana dApp Store.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Wallet Address: When you connect a Solana wallet, we receive your public wallet address. This is a public blockchain address and does not contain private or personal information.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Basic device identifiers for crash reporting and analytics (device type, OS version, app version).
  • Usage Data: Anonymous statistics about app features usage to improve user experience.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Private Keys: We never have access to your wallet's private keys. All signing operations are performed through the Mobile Wallet Adapter protocol in your external wallet app.
  • Seed Phrases: We never request, collect, or store recovery phrases.
  • Personal Identity: We do not require or collect names, emails, phone numbers, or other personally identifiable information.
  • Location Data: We do not collect precise location data.
  • Contacts: We do not access your contact list.

How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Display your wallet balance and token holdings
  • Process transactions you authorize through your wallet
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Provide customer support when requested

Blockchain Data

Please note that the Solana blockchain is a public distributed ledger. Any transactions you sign and submit become publicly visible and permanently recorded on the blockchain. This is inherent to blockchain technology and not within our control.

Third-Party Services

The App may interact with:

  • Solana RPC Nodes: To query blockchain data (balances, transactions)
  • Mobile Wallet Adapter: To connect with wallet applications you have installed

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • All network communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • No sensitive data is stored locally without encryption
  • We follow security best practices for mobile applications

Data Retention

  • Cached Data: Temporary cache data (balances, token info) is stored locally on your device and cleared when you uninstall the app
  • Analytics Data: Anonymous usage statistics may be retained for up to 12 months

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Disconnect: Disconnect your wallet at any time through the app settings
  • Delete: Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data
  • Access: Request information about what data we have about your wallet address

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date and, for significant changes, through an in-app notification.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: contact@asdf-ecosystem.com
  • Website: https://asdf-web.onrender.com

Summary: ASDF does not access your private keys, does not collect personal information, and does not sell your data. We only interact with public blockchain data through your authorized wallet connections.
